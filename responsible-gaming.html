Responsible Gaming Policy

At UBettor.ag, we are committed to providing a safe, enjoyable, and responsible gaming experience. While gambling can be a thrilling form of entertainment, it is essential to recognize that it can become problematic for some individuals. When gambling begins to negatively affect personal well-being, finances, or relationships, we encourage players to seek support and practice responsible gaming.

We believe that gaming should be fun, and we are here to help you keep it that way.

Understanding Responsible Gaming

Gambling should always be viewed as a recreational activity. However, when the fun stops, or if gambling starts to feel like an obligation or source of stress, it is time to reassess. Recognizing the signs early is key to preventing harm.

Ask yourself:

  • Do you use gambling to escape from stress, boredom, or other problems?
  • Do you gamble with money you can't afford to lose?
  • When you lose, do you feel compelled to chase your losses?
  • Do you gamble more than you intended, or lose track of time while gambling?
  • Has gambling caused you to neglect personal responsibilities or relationships?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions, it may be a sign that you need to take a step back and reassess your relationship with gambling. We encourage you to consider the resources we provide for those who may need support.

Guidelines for Responsible Gaming

To ensure that gambling remains a positive experience, we suggest the following principles:

Treat Gambling as Entertainment

Always approach gambling as a form of entertainment, not a means to make money. Remember that with any form of gambling, there is always a risk of losing. Only gamble with money you can afford to lose.

Set Limits and Stick to Them

Set a clear budget before you begin gambling. Decide on both time and financial limits. Once you've hit your limit, walk away. Gambling should never interfere with other responsibilities or areas of your life.

Take Breaks When Needed

If you feel that gambling is taking up too much of your time, take a break. Setting aside designated "gambling-free" time can help you maintain balance and perspective.

Do Not Chase Losses

Losing can be part of the gambling experience, but chasing losses can lead to bigger problems. Never increase your bets or try to recoup losses by continuing to gamble.

Stay in Control of Your Emotions

Avoid gambling when you are upset, anxious, or stressed. Making decisions when in a negative emotional state can lead to poor outcomes and unhealthy patterns.

Support and Assistance for Problem Gambling

If you believe that gambling is having a negative impact on your life or if you notice any signs of problem gambling, it's important to seek help. UBettor.ag is here to support you, and we provide access to trusted organizations offering guidance and assistance:

Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org

Gambling Therapy: www.gamblingtherapy.org

These organizations offer global support, including free advice and emotional assistance, 24/7.

Taking Control: Self-Exclusion and Time-Out Options

UBettor.ag offers tools to help you manage your gambling habits if you need a break or if you decide to stop gambling entirely.

Time-Out: A Temporary Break

If you feel you need to step away for a while, you can request a time-out. This will temporarily suspend your account, giving you time to reflect and reset. Your funds and ungraded bets will remain unaffected during this period.

Self-Exclusion: A Permanent Solution

If you believe you need a longer-term solution, you can opt for self-exclusion. This will permanently close your account, and you will not be able to access any of our services. Additionally, you will stop receiving marketing materials, ensuring that you can begin the process of distancing yourself from gambling entirely.

To request a time-out or self-exclusion, please reach out to our support team. We are here to assist you in any way we can.

Protecting Yourself

We understand that personal circumstances can change, and sometimes gambling can go from being fun to a harmful behavior. If you ever feel that your gambling is becoming problematic or impacting your life, take immediate action. Reach out for support, take a break, and set the necessary boundaries to ensure you maintain control.

At UBettor.ag, we are dedicated to offering a safe, enjoyable experience, and we encourage all players to play responsibly.
